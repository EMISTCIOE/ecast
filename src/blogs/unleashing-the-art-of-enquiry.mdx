### Blog: Unleashing the Art of Inquiry – A Creative Approach to Research

On **21st June 2023**, the halls of **Thapathali Campus** buzzed with intellectual energy as scholars, students, and research enthusiasts gathered for an extraordinary seminar, *"𝗨𝗻𝗹𝗲𝗮𝘀𝗵𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝘁𝗵𝗲 𝗔𝗿𝘁 𝗼𝗳 𝗜𝗻𝗾𝘂𝗶𝗿𝘆: 𝗘𝘅𝗽𝗹𝗼𝗿𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗥𝗲𝘀𝗲𝗮𝗿𝗰𝗵 𝗮𝘀 𝗮 𝗖𝗿𝗲𝗮𝘁𝗶𝘃𝗲 𝗣𝗿𝗮𝗰𝘁𝗶𝗰𝗲."* Hosted in **A Hall**, the event welcomed participants eager to break free from traditional notions of research and explore its artistic dimensions.

#### The Power of Creativity in Academia

The seminar was headlined by the illustrious **𝗥𝗮𝗰𝗵𝗮𝗲𝗹 𝗟𝗮𝘂**, whose inspiring words encouraged attendees to view research as more than systematic analysis. **"Research isn’t just a process; it’s an art,"** Lau emphasized, urging participants to embrace curiosity and creative thinking in their academic endeavors.

#### Key Highlights from the Seminar

- **Reimagining Research**  
   Lau challenged the audience to rethink research beyond rigid methodologies, highlighting the transformative potential of curiosity-driven exploration.

- **The Role of Creativity in Inquiry**  
   Through captivating examples, the session explored how creativity fosters innovation, especially in interdisciplinary studies, making research more impactful and fulfilling.

- **Engaging Activities**  
   Participants engaged in brainstorming exercises that demonstrated how creative perspectives could redefine their approach to formulating and solving research questions.

- **Interactive Q&A Session**  
   The seminar concluded with an engaging Q&A, where attendees sought advice on blending artistic intuition with academic rigor.

#### A Transformative Experience

The event resonated deeply with participants. For many, it was a pivotal moment that redefined their perception of research. **"This seminar changed how I see my thesis project,"** remarked one attendee, **"I now feel inspired to explore beyond traditional frameworks."**

#### Looking Ahead

The success of *"Unleashing the Art of Inquiry"* highlights the growing need to foster creativity in academic spaces. Such events not only enrich our understanding of research but also inspire a new generation of scholars to innovate and think differently.
